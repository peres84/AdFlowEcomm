# Images Directory

## Landing Page Video

Place your landing page background video here as:
- `landing-page-video.mp4`

The video will automatically play as a background in the hero section of the landing page.

### Video Requirements:
- Format: MP4
- Recommended resolution: 1920x1080 or higher
- The video will be displayed with dark overlays for text readability
- It will autoplay, loop, and be muted

### Fallback:
If the video file is not present, a gradient background will be shown instead.
